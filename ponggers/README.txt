Low quality Pong clone.

made by:
	Gentleman Duck

[  ATTENTION  ]:
	I recommend lowering the volume, as the sfx are really loud.

Controls:
	player 1 : [W] and [S] to move 
	player 2 : [UP] and [DOWN] to move
	[ ESC ] to open menu
